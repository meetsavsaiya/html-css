Mercedes Benz

Mercedes Benz is a German multinational automobile manufacturer and a global automotive luxury vehicle brand. The company is a subsidiary of Daimler AG and is one of the world's largest automobile manufacturers. The brand's logo, which features a stylized Mercedes logo and a blue "S" symbol, is often used as a symbol of luxury and sophistication in the automotive industry.
